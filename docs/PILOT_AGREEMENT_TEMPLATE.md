## Statement of Work (SOW) Template

**PILOT STATEMENT OF WORK**

**Between**: GridVision AI, Inc. ("Provider")  
**And**: [CUSTOMER COMPANY NAME] ("Customer")  
**Effective Date**: [DATE]  
**Pilot Period**: 30 days

---

### 1. SCOPE OF WORK

Provider will provide access to GridVision AI platform for Customer's evaluation of capacity planning and grid analysis capabilities.

**Services Include**:
- Access to cloud-based GridVision AI platform
- Professional tier subscription (unlimited users, 1,000 assets, 500 scenarios)
- Up to 5 hours of onboarding and training
- Weekly support calls with GridVision team
- Data import assistance (CSV upload)
- Access to demo data and documentation

**Deliverables**:
- Platform access within 48 hours of SOW execution
- Onboarding completion by Day 3
- First scenario model completion by Day 10
- ROI validation report by Day 20
- Pilot feedback survey by Day 28

---

### 2. CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES

Customer will:
- Provide necessary data files (substation, transformer, feeder inventories)
- Designate primary contact for GridVision communication
- Allocate 5-10 hours of internal time for pilot activities
- Provide feedback via survey and evaluation forms
- Complete evaluation and communicate decision by Day 32

---

### 3. PRICING & PAYMENT

**Pilot Fee**: $0 (free evaluation)

If Customer signs a production subscription within 30 days of pilot end date:
- Grid Vision will provide 50% discount on first month's subscription
- Production subscription starts Day 1 of Month 2

---

### 4. DATA & CONFIDENTIALITY

- Customer retains all rights to data provided to Provider
- Provider will not use Customer data for marketing without written consent
- Both parties agree to keep pilot status confidential for 60 days
- Data will be deleted 30 days after pilot end unless Customer converts to paid subscription

---

### 5. SUCCESS CRITERIA

Pilot will be considered successful if:

✅ **Technical Success**:
- GridVision platform is operational and accessible
- Customer can import 80%+ of asset data
- At least 2 scenarios successfully modeled
- No critical bugs or data loss

✅ **Business Success**:
- Planning team provides feedback score ≥7/10
- ROI calculation shows ≥$100K annual savings potential
- Minimum 2 team members complete training
- Feedback survey response rate ≥80%

✅ **Organizational Success**:
- Executive sponsor confirms problem/solution alignment
- Approval to proceed with production deployment
- Budget confirmed for annual subscription

---

### 6. SUPPORT & SLAs (PILOT TERMS)

**Business Hours Support**: 9 AM-5 PM ET, Monday-Friday  
**Response Time**: <24 hours for non-critical issues  
**Resolution Target**: <7 days for issues not blocking pilot  
**Escalation**: [GridVision contact] can escalate to VP Product

---

### 7. TERMINATION & CONVERSIONS

**Pilot Termination** (If Customer declines to convert):
- Access disabled 30 days after pilot end date
- All Customer data deleted
- No further support or access

**Pilot Conversion** (If Customer signs production contract):
- Pilot credit applied: 50% off Month 1 subscription
- Production contract begins on Day 1 of Month 2
- Data retained in production environment
- Ongoing support per production SLA

---

### 8. LIMITATIONS & DISCLAIMERS

- GridVision platform provided "as-is" without warranties
