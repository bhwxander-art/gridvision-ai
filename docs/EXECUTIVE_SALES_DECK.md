**Visual Theme**:
- Modern, clean design (think Tesla, not legacy utility software)

- Each slide focuses on ONE idea
- Real screenshots from product > generic diagrams

- Primary: Deep blue (#0066cc) + energy orange (#ff6600)
- Neutrals: White, gray

## Delivery Tips

1. **Open with problem**: "How many of you have experienced interconnection delays?"
2. **Stay focused**: Don't oversell features; focus on time + cost savings
3. **Use numbers**: $20M, 40% faster, $72K (very different from $500K)
4. **Demo if possible**: 5-minute live walkthrough beats 10 slides
5. **Close early**: "Is this something you'd like to explore?" (Slide 14)
6. **Provide takeaway**: Leave the ROI calculator URL + 30-day offer
